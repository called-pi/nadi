A quiet corner of the internet for when things feel heavy.  
No sign-up. No tracking. Just you, and a little breathing room.
The door wide opened https://called-pi.github.io/nadi/

---

## What's inside

| Section | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Breathe** | Guided 4-7-8 breathing animation to help you slow down |
| **Release** | Write what's weighing on you — then burn it. Gone. |
| **Care** | Tend to a little four-leaf clover. Water it, light it up, clean its leaves. |
| **The Nice Place** | A warm corner of the internet, full of hugs from strangers. |

---

## Tech

Pure HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript — no frameworks, no build tools.  
Canvas-based particle systems for fire and plant-care effects.  
All text in the textarea stays local in your browser and is never sent anywhere.
